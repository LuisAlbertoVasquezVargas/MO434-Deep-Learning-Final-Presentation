\section{Introduction and Motivation}

\begin{frame}{Context}
  \small
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Deep learning models can achieve strong performance in visual recognition tasks.
    \item Larger models often require more computational resources for training and inference.
    \item The final project studies this trade-off through controlled experiments and quantitative evaluation.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Motivation}
  \small
  \begin{alertblock}{Main Motivation}
    Understand how different deep learning training strategies affect accuracy, generalization, and computational cost.
  \end{alertblock}

  \vspace{0.3cm}

  \begin{itemize}
    \item Compare baseline and improved training configurations.
    \item Analyze model behavior across datasets.
    \item Identify practical limitations and future improvements.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
